Overview

Paying guest accommodations, commonly referred to as PGs, are residential arrangements where individuals rent rooms or shared spaces within a private home or dedicated facility. In exchange for rent, residents receive lodging along with certain amenities and services.PG accommodations come in various types, including single-room PGs, shared-room PGs, gender-specific PGs, working professional PGs, student PGs.

PG accommodations are often found in urban centers, near educational institutions, job hubs, medical centers, transportation hubs, and tourist destinations. The availability of PGs in specific locations depends on factors like demand and proximity to amenities.
